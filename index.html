Crystal Collector


You Will be Given a Random Number Goal at the Start of the Game. Each of the Crystals Below Has a Value That Will be Added to Your Total Score.

You Will Win the Game By Matching Your Total Score with the Number Goal, You Lose if Your Total Score Goes Over the Number Goal.

The Value of Each Crystal is Hidden Until You Click on it to Find Out. Each Time a New Game Starts the Crystals Will be Given New Values.
